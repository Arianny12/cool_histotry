Amazon creator: Jeff Bezos

Day of creation: July 5, 1994

Amazon's net worth 2022: $984.87 billion USD

Jeff Bezos net worth 2022: $115.8 billion USD

Interesting Facts

  • Amazon was a website that only sold books.
  • In the mid to late 90s Amazon started doing same-day delivery which was the first time in the world where you can do same-day delivery.
  • Jeff Bezos expected employees to work at least 60 hours per week, where the idea of work-life balance didn't exist.
  • First-ever order was placed on April 3 of 1995
  • On July 16, 1995, Amazon officially opens for business as an online bookseller. Within a month, the fledgling retailer had shipped books to all 50 U.S. states and to 45 countries.
  • Bezos favored the name "Relentless" if you visit Relentless.com today, it navigates to Amazon.
  • He initially wanted the business to be named Cadabra (as in abracadabra) but after someone misheard the name as “cadaver”, Bezos decided to call his startup Amazon.

How it was created

Amazon was founded in the garage of Bezos' rented home in Bellevue, Washington. Bezos' parents invested almost $250,000 in the start-up. On July 16, 1995, Amazon opened as an online bookseller, selling the world's largest collection of books to anyone with World Wide Web access.
